## 这是什么？

本书是对古代印度哲学家 **龙树菩萨**（约公元 150–250 年）所著《中论》（*Mūlamadhyamakakārikā*）的现代汉语全译本。

《中论》是佛教哲学最深奥的著作之一，核心论证一切事物的"空性"（śūnyatā）。但现存鸠摩罗什汉译本（公元 5 世纪）夹杂大量评注，且用语古奥，令当代读者难以接近。本项目尝试做三件事：

1. **提取纯净原文** —— 去除鸠摩罗什与青目的评注，只保留龙树本人的偈颂
2. **现代逐句翻译** —— 用现代汉语重新翻译，还原龙树的论证逻辑
3. **实验性跨学科注释** —— 以当代哲学概念（现象学、系统论、认知科学等）为参照，探索龙树思想的可能当代回响

**关于注释的说明：** 书中的跨学科重构注释目前处于探索阶段，尚未经过严格的学术审校，不应被视为权威的学术论述。欢迎读者提出批评与修正。

## What Is This?

This is a modern Chinese complete translation of the *Mūlamadhyamakakārikā* (Fundamental Verses on the Middle Way) by the ancient Indian philosopher **Nāgārjuna** (c. 150–250 CE).

The *Mūlamadhyamakārikā* is one of the most profound works in Buddhist philosophy, presenting arguments for the "emptiness" (śūnyatā) of all phenomena. However, the existing Chinese translation by Kumārajīva (5th century) is interwoven with extensive commentaries that make it difficult for contemporary readers to access. This project attempts three things:

1. **Extracting pure original text** — removing the commentaries by Kumārajīva and Piṅgala, preserving only Nāgārjuna's own verses
2. **Modern line-by-line translation** — re-translating into modern Chinese to reconstruct Nāgārjuna's argumentative logic
3. **Experimental interdisciplinary commentary** — exploring possible contemporary resonances of Nāgārjuna's thought using concepts from phenomenology, systems theory, cognitive science, etc.

**Note on commentary:** The interdisciplinary reconstructions in this book are currently exploratory and have not undergone rigorous academic review. They should not be taken as authoritative scholarly statements. Reader feedback and corrections are welcome.
